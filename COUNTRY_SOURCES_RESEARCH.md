## Research Methodology

### Step 1: Official Government Portals
Search official government websites for:
- Ministry of Finance / Treasury
- Civil Service Commission
- Public Service Department
- Travel expense regulations

### Step 2: Legal/Regulatory Documents
Look for:
- Laws on public servant travel
- Ministerial decrees/circulars
- Budget/financial regulations
- Official gazettes

### Step 3: Translation
For non-English sources:
- Use official English versions when available
- Machine translate regulation titles
- Document original language terms

### Step 4: Data Extraction
For each country, extract:
- Daily lodging allowance by destination country
- Daily meals/subsistence by destination
- Currency (usually home currency or USD/EUR)
- Update frequency
- Official source URL
- Last update date

### Step 5: Verification
- Cross-check with multiple sources
- Verify publication dates
- Check for amendments/updates
- Note any seasonal variations

## Challenges Identified

1. **Language Barriers:** Many sources only in local languages
2. **Access Restrictions:** Some data internal/not publicly published
3. **Format Variety:** PDFs, spreadsheets, legal texts
4. **Update Frequency:** Varies widely by country
5. **Complexity:** Different rates by rank, purpose, region
6. **Decentralization:** Some countries let departments set own rates
